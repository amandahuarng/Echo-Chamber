Current Mood Playlist Generator

Use audio features to determine the "mood" of a given track. Audio features are a method of quantifying the aspects of different songs in order to better categorize them and use them to bring desired music to the user.

The audio features include valence, acousticness, danceability, energy, instrumentalness, liveness, and speechiness, duration time, tempo, and key.

To find these audio features, we use the Spotify API's Get Audio Features for Several Tracks endpoint.
